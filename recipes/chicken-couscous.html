Chicken and Couscous

Description

Chicken and couscous, a staple of summertime eating. Fitness-fanatic approved. Couscous is fun to say. What isn't there to love?

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of trimmed chicken breast
  • 1 box of Far East couscous
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over a medium heat.
  2. Butterfly and season chicken breasts; add to hot pan; cook 5 minutes a side.
  3. Boil 1 1/4 cups of water and contents of couscous' spice pack. Once boiling, remove from heat and add in couscous. Let stand, covered for 5 minutes.
  4. Combine and enjoy.
