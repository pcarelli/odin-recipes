Ice Cream, straight from the container

Description

Are you avoiding getting ready for bed and wandering into the kitchen? Are you adequetely full but not quite "I hate myself full"? Well then let me introduce you to Ice Cream, straight from the container. No need for a bowl, or even a spoon for that matter, any clean silverware in the drawer will do.

You will surely regret this in 15 minutes while laying in bed trying to fall asleep.

Ingredients

  • Pint (or larger) of ice cream
  • Any clean utensil
  • No respect for hunger limits

Steps

  1. Open the freezer door and leave it open, this will only take...5 seconds (minutes).
  2. Place ice cream on counter, scavenge silverware drawer for any clean utensil.
  3. Stand next to fridge shoveling ice cream into your mouth.
  4. Tell yourself "one more bite".
  5. Take 3 more bites.
  6. Return ice cream to the freezer.
  7. Regret.
